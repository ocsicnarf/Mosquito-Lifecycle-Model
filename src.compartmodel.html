update_transition(from_name, to_name, prob)[source]¶

Updates a transition between two model compartments, and rescales the outgoing transition probabilities of the “from” compartment.

Parameters:
  • from_name – The transition starts at the model compartment with this name.
  • to_name – The transition ends at the model compartment with this name.
  • prob – The transition probability.
Returns:

None.

Note

The transition need not have been explicitly set before. If the given transition probability is 1, then all other outgoing transition probabilities are rescaled to 0, and their weights relative to each other are lost.
